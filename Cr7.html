Roman Reigns

Early Life

Leati Joseph Anoa'i was born on May 25,1985,in Pensacola,Florida,,to sika Anoa'i and Patricia Hooker.His father is of Samoan descent and his mother is of Sicilian ancestry.Both his father and his brother Roseywere proffesioanl wrestlers.As a member of the Anoa'i wrestling family,his cousins include The Tonga Kid,Rikishi,Umaga,and Yokozuna.

Personal Life

In early 2005,while studying at Georgia Institute of Technology,Anoa'i met and began dating Galina Becker.They were married in December 2014,and reside in Tampa,Florida.Their first child,a daughter was born in December 2007 and appeared with Anoa'i in a PSA in June 2014.They had twins sons in November 2016,followed by another set of twins sons in March 2020. At a house show on Ddecember 30,2015,Anoa'i had his nose legitimately broken during his match against sheamus,and underwent nasal reconstructive surgery on February 23,2016. In 2018,Anoa'i shared that he had been living with leukamia,an incurable but treatable form of blood cancer.He was diagnosed in 2007 during his American Football career and continues lifelong oral chemotherapy to manage the condition

The Bloodline

The Bloodline is a villanious proffessional wrestling stable that performs in WWWE on the SmackDown brand.The team is currently led by Solo Sikoa and features The Rock,The Tongans(Tama Tonga,Tonga Loa),and Jacob Fatu,and was formerly led by Roman Reigns.Sikoa serves as the acting leader following hiatuses by the Rock and Reigns,both beginning in reference to the majority of the group being members of the Anoa'i family of Samoans proffessional wrestlers.
